HOW DO YOU LEARN TO BOX? IN 6 STAPS

1 Front toe & back heel on the centre line. ... 2 Weight evenly distributed across both legs, knees slightly bent. 3 Feet diagonal, little wider than shoulder-width apart, back heel raised. 4 Elbows down, hands up. 5 Head behind your gloves, chin slightly down, eyes see over the gloves. 6 Relax and breathe!
